Features

  • Vetted, high-quality tutors

    Get privately tutored by verified tutors studying in premier colleges, hand-picked through a rigorous testing process. Our tutors are from IIT-Delhi, IIT-Varanasi, BITS Pilani-Pilani campus and BITS Pilani-Hyderabad campus.

  • Affordable

    No complicated plans, we offer a simple pay-as-you-use model at a price 50% lower than a conventional private tutor. Top your account with as little as Rs.100. Now everyone truly can afford a high-quality personal tutor!

  • On-demand, on your mobile

    Stuck on a concept at 2 am? No problem. Connect to your own HashLearn Now tutor instantly at the click of a button. Our simple chat-interface offers you a focused, distraction-free learning environment right on your mobile.

  • Personalized, at your pace

    Our tutors work at your pace and on topics of your choice. Tutors are selected at a topic-level to ensure the best fit for the student, unlike coaching centres where the same professors usually cover all topics within a subject.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get HashLearn Now?

    Instant Tutoring on HashLearn Now is being launched exclusively on the 15 June 2015. The App will be available for download on the google play, but to access instant tutoring you will need an invite. If you don’t have an invite, you can subscribe for one here.

  • How do I schedule a lesson on HashLearn Now?

    Login to the app. Enter your invite code (more on invites here), you will be taken to the Home screen. From the Home Screen, select the topic relevant to the question you want answered. (For example, Alkanes in Chemistry or Coordinate Geometry in Mathematics). You could choose to directly connect to a tutor by clicking on the 'Search for tutor' button. Alternatively, you could upload a picture of the question [Upload directly from your Gallery or click a picture then] and then select the 'Search for tutor' option. We'll connect you to the tutors currently active on HashLearn Now, instantly!

  • How much do I pay for a lesson on HashLearn Now?

    Multiple subscription plans will be made available to all HashLearn Now users to suit their needs. Users can also pay as they as use on a per minute basis. Note that this rate will vary if you have availed for a subscription plan.

  • Can I get admission help on HashLearn Now?

    Yes, you can get stream and campus specific help on HashLearn Now. As a part of our exclusive launch we are only providing admission help relevant to the BITS Pilani Campus. However you can still avail stream specific help.

  • Is my device supported?

    HashLearn Now is supported on any Android Device running Jellybean OS or above.

  • How do I pay for my lessons on HashLearn Now?

    HashLearn Now uses PayU, a closed Digital Wallet service similar to services like PayTM and Ola. You should top up the wallet using your debit/credit card and the amount charged is automatically deducted from your wallet after a session. Swipe right on the home screen (or) Click on the drawer button on the top-right corner of the screen to open up your sliding drawer. Once it appears, select the 'My Wallet' option to take you to the recharge screen.

  • What is the refund policy if I'm not satisfied with my lesson?

    You get charged by the minute and are charged once the lesson is completed. If an issue is reported within 24 hours of session completion, our customer Support team will go through the transcripts of the lesson in question and will refund the full amount if serious discrepancy is seen. You could report the issue via the app or drop us a mail at support@HashLearnNow.com and we’ll get back to you.
